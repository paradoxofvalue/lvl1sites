Welcome to Super New York Painters

We are a family owned and operated painting company servicing all of Clark County. From residential to commercial projects, our team is committed to providing timely, professional and dependable interior and exterior painting services.

Super New York Painters offers you a complete line of high quality home interior painting and professional paint-related services. Our painting expertise and our superior service truly stand out from other painting contractors in New York.

When preparing to paint the interior of your home or workspace, we take the steps necessary to protect the area and ready each surface prior to application of the primer and finish coats. Our Super New York Painters professionals will cover your belongings and furnishings to protect them from paint and trade-related debris, and will clean up after themselves at the end of each day.

Our team is very friendly, neat, clean, fully experienced and knowledgeable. Their behavior is always professional, respectful and non-disruptive.

An exterior paint job in New York can have a huge affect on your home’s visual appeal and value. Paint is an often-overlooked aspect of a home – and of all major renovations and improvements you can make, new paint often brings you the largest return on your investment.

In addition to doing beautiful work, Super New York Painters's team brings the proper equipment and expertise to renew the exterior of your home. Our skilled team of professional painters have extensive knowledge in both materials and application methods.

We don’t skip steps in preparing surfaces to be painted, and we are careful to protect and cover the areas not to be painted. Attention to detail and safety are integral elements of our methods, and we only use high-quality paints because we want to be as proud of our work as you are of your home’s new appearance.

Super New York Painters is a Top Rated New York commercial painting contractor specializing in home owner associations and apartment complexes. Our combination of trained craftsmen and quality paints brings you the finest in exterior finishes at the most competitive pricing available!

We’ll be happy to answer your questions, provide estimates, and give you more information about why we are the best choice for your project. We provide the same level of service and care for home owner associations and property management companies that we do for private residences.

Plus, we offer power washing, trim revitalization, and full painting-related repair services.
